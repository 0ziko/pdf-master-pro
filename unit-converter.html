FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Which unit systems does it support?
The converter supports metric (SI), imperial, and US customary systems. This covers units like kilometers, miles, kilograms, pounds, Celsius, Fahrenheit, Kelvin, liters, gallons, and many more across all seven categories.
How accurate are the conversions?
Conversions are calculated with up to 10 significant figures of precision using JavaScript's double-precision floating-point arithmetic — accurate enough for virtually all practical, scientific, and engineering applications.
Can I convert temperature?
Yes. The Temperature category supports Celsius (°C), Fahrenheit (°F), and Kelvin (K) with exact conversion formulas — not approximations. Switch to the Temperature tab and select your units.
Is there a limit to what I can convert?
There are no practical limits. The tool handles very small and very large numbers equally well. Simply type in your value and the conversion result appears instantly — no upper or lower bound restrictions.
Does this tool work offline?
Once the page is loaded, all conversions run entirely in your browser with no server calls. If you already have the page open, it will continue working even if your internet connection drops.
