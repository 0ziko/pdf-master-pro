FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I reduce my PDF file size?
Depending on content, you can reduce PDF size by 30–80%. PDFs with many embedded images compress the most. Text-only PDFs may see 10–30% reduction. Use the Low quality setting for the smallest possible file size.
Is my PDF uploaded to any server?
No. Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser using JavaScript (PDF.js + PDF-lib). Nothing is ever sent to our servers — your documents stay completely private on your device.
Will compression reduce text quality or readability?
Compression converts PDF pages to JPEG images, so text becomes part of an image. At normal zoom (100%) it looks fine. If you need fully searchable text, use the High quality setting which preserves more detail.
What is the maximum file size I can compress?
The tool handles PDFs up to 100 MB. For very large files, your browser may need a few seconds to process each page. Large PDFs with 100+ pages will still work, just allow a moment for processing.
Can I compress a password-protected PDF?
Currently the compressor works on unprotected PDFs. To compress a protected PDF, first use our Decrypt PDF tool to remove the password, then compress it.
