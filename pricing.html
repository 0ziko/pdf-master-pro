Frequently Asked Questions

Is SnakeConverter really free? ▾
Yes, all 50+ tools are completely free with no time limit, no account, and no credit card. We'll always keep a generous free tier.
When will Pro launch? ▾
We're actively working on it. Join the waitlist above and you'll be the first to know — and get a founding member discount.
Do you store my files? ▾
No. All file processing happens entirely in your browser. Nothing is uploaded to our servers unless you explicitly use the PDF Share feature, which stores files encrypted in Cloudflare R2 and deletes them automatically after 7 days (30 days for Pro).
What payment methods will Pro accept? ▾
We'll accept all major credit and debit cards, and PayPal, via Stripe's secure checkout. No payment data ever touches our servers.
Can I cancel Pro anytime? ▾
Absolutely. Cancel any time from your account settings and you won't be charged again. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
