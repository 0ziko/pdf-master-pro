FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I save the invoice as PDF?
Yes. Click the Print / Save as PDF button to open your browser's print dialog. In the destination dropdown, select Save as PDF and click Save. Works in Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.
Is my invoice data stored anywhere?
No. Everything stays entirely in your browser. No data is ever sent to any server — your invoice details, client information, and amounts are 100% private on your device.
Can I add tax and discount?
Yes. The invoice generator has both a tax rate (%) field and a discount percentage field. Enter the values and the subtotal, discount amount, tax amount, and grand total are calculated automatically in real time.
What currencies are supported?
Six currencies are supported: USD ($), EUR (€), GBP (£), TRY (₺), JPY (¥), and CAD (CA$). Select your currency from the dropdown and all amounts will display the correct symbol on both the form and the printed invoice.
Can I reuse the invoice?
Currently each session starts fresh. Use the browser print dialog to save the invoice as a PDF for your records. Then click Reset to clear the form and fill in new details for your next invoice.
